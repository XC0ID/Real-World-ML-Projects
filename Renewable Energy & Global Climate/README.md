# 🌍 Renewable Energy & Global Climate Analysis

A data science project exploring the relationship between **renewable energy production, climate indicators, and global sustainability trends**.

This project is part of the **Core ML Playground**, a collection of machine learning and data analysis experiments focused on real-world datasets.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The global climate crisis has accelerated the transition toward renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydro power. Understanding how these energy sources interact with climate variables is essential for improving sustainability and planning future energy systems.

This project analyzes renewable energy datasets to uncover patterns, trends, and insights about global climate and energy consumption.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

• Analyze renewable energy production trends across regions
• Explore climate variables affecting renewable energy output
• Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) on global climate datasets
• Build predictive or analytical models to understand energy trends
• Visualize climate and energy relationships through clear graphs
