# Renewable Energy & Global Climate Analysis

## Project Report

---

# 1. Introduction

Climate change has become one of the most significant global challenges of the 21st century. The increase in greenhouse gas emissions from fossil fuel usage has led to rising global temperatures and environmental instability.

Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, hydroelectric, and geothermal energy provide sustainable alternatives that reduce carbon emissions and support long-term environmental stability.

This project focuses on analyzing renewable energy data and climate indicators to understand trends and relationships that influence sustainable energy systems.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Energy demand continues to rise worldwide, while climate change pressures governments and industries to reduce emissions.

Key questions explored in this project:

* How is renewable energy production evolving globally?
* What climate variables affect renewable energy output?
* How can data analysis help understand sustainability trends?

---

# 3. Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

• Analyze global renewable energy datasets
• Identify relationships between climate factors and energy production
• Visualize energy and environmental trends
• Provide insights that support sustainable energy planning

---

# 4. Dataset Description

The dataset contains environmental and energy-related information including:

These variables allow the analysis of how climate conditions and policy trends influence renewable energy usage.
